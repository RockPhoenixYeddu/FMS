PROBLEM STATEMENT

Managing organisations and Business finances using tools like Excel can quickly become confusing and time-consuming for non-technical users. Manual entry, complex formulas, poor navigation, and lack of visual summaries often lead to mistakes and difficulty in tracking spending patterns.
SOLUTION
There is a need for a simple, user-friendly finance management system that allows users to record, view, and analyze their income and expenses without requiring spreadsheet knowledge or technical expertise.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Heads and Admins of Non-Profit Orgs and businesses


FEATURES

0) MERN stack, 3 tier MVC Architecture
1) Admin driven use
2) Well Detailed Login and Registry with Encryption
3) User Friendly but powerful and Well organised UI
4) Automatic Calculations
5) Flexible PDF print
6) Detailed Transaction Records with Proofs
7) Immediate Updates
8) Add, Edit, Delete, Search

PROS

1) Well Organised
2) Digitalized Work
3) Tax benefits
4) Transparency
5) High Security
6) Weighted Proofs

CONS

1) Cant Hide Black money
2) Needs constant updates to catch up
3) Initial Costs of Marketing
